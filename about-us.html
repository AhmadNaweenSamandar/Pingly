What is Pingly?

Pingly is a platform designed to bridge the gap between students and recruiters in Ottawa. We provide a space where innovative ideas meet skilled collaborators, and where talents are discovered and nurtured. Our mission is to create a thriving ecosystem for tech enthusiasts and professionals alike.

What do we change?

We're transforming how students showcase their skills and how recruiters find talent. By focusing on real-world project collaboration and peer learning, we're moving beyond traditional resumes and grades to a more holistic approach to talent evaluation and career growth.
