What is our goal?

Our goal is to create a vibrant community of students. By providing a space to post project ideas, ask questions, and engage in live coding, we aim to make learning more interactive and supportive.

We want to empower students by rewarding their contributions with points that reflect their skills and experience level. This recognition motivates participation and also builds a trusted record.

At the same time, we connect students with recruiters who are searching for talent based on proven collaboration and coding experience. Our ultimate goal is to bridge the gap between learning and career opportunities.

What is Pingly?

Pingly is a platform designed to bridge the gap between students and recruiters in Ottawa. We provide a space where innovative ideas meet skilled collaborators, and where talents are discovered and nurtured. Our mission is to create a thriving ecosystem for tech enthusiasts and professionals alike.

What do we change?

We're transforming how students showcase their skills and how recruiters find talent. By focusing on real-world project collaboration and peer learning, we're moving beyond traditional resumes and grades to a more holistic approach to talent evaluation and career growth.
